Blue rose-A musical fantasy, to hit the screens soon
Blue rose is a musical fantasy by a bunch of talented youngsters starring Neha. It portrays the journey of a music enthusiast who derives inspiration from the host of a music-centric television show. The featured song composed by Brindly Sujith has been visualized under Gokul Narayanan's directorship. Ranjith Pillai dons the producer's hat. Co-founder and lead singer at Masala Coffee, Sooraj Santhosh and Divyavalli Santhosh has lend their soulful voices to this musical treat. The video narrates the journey of music enthusiast Alan who derives inspiration from Rose Ann, a television music programme anchor. Brindly plays Alan. Neha potrays Rose Ann and is really looking forward its release.
